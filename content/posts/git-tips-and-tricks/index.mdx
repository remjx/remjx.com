This post contains links I've found helpful when working with [Git](https://git-scm.com/) version control.

- [Branching standards & conventions by @digitaljhelms](https://gist.github.com/digitaljhelms/4287848). Advocates for a `stable` and `master` being the only two persistent branches. `feature/`s and `bug/`s branch from & get merged back to `master`. `hotfix/`s branch from `stable` and get merged back into `stable` and `master`.
